PS04 - Domestic Violence Support and Legal Aid System

Victims of domestic violence often struggle to access confidential support and accurate legal information. This secure Django-based application provides a safe space to seek help, counseling, and legal guidance.

Problem Description

The platform prioritizes privacy, data encryption, and controlled access. It connects survivors with professionals while ensuring anonymity and safety.

Core Features

Secure help request system

Survivors can submit confidential support requests through a protected workflow.

Counseling and legal resources

Users can access guidance and support materials from professionals and administrators.

Confidential communication

Private interaction keeps communication limited to authorized roles only.

Roles

Admin, Survivor, Counsellor, Legal Advisor

Description of the Roles

  • Admin: Maintains platform security and content
  • Survivor: Accesses resources and seeks help
  • Counsellor: Provides emotional and psychological support
  • Legal Advisor: Offers legal advice and assistance
